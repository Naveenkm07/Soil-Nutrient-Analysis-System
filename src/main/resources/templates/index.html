💡 How to Use

1. Enter your region or location to get region-specific recommendations

2. Input your soil test results for Nitrogen (N), Phosphorus (P), and Potassium (K)

3. Provide your soil pH level (typically between 5.0 and 8.5)

4. Click "Analyze Soil" to get personalized fertilizer recommendations

📈 Understanding Your Results

Nitrogen (N): Essential for leaf growth and green color. Low levels may indicate need for nitrogen-rich fertilizers.

Phosphorus (P): Important for root development and flowering. Critical for early plant growth.

Potassium (K): Helps with disease resistance and overall plant health. Important for fruit and seed production.

pH Level: Affects nutrient availability. Most plants prefer pH between 6.0 and 7.5.
